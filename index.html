Madridista

Madridista is a quite famous nickname of the club of fans - the fans of Real Madrid. There is no exact concept as well as specific information to describe the meaning of the word Madridista. Each name and nickname has its own meaning. The name Madridista refers to the true fans who have a great love for the Real Madrid club. Sticking with the team from year to year, when they succeed or fail, Madridista is always on the side.
